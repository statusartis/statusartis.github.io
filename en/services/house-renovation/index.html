House and business space renovations

At Status Artis we undertake all types of renovation and renovation of homes and businesses. We have perfectly coordinated workshops in order to complete the work in the shortest possible time. This is also the most important issue in the renovations, taking into account your needs for the short reopening of the space to be renovated.

In the context of our cooperation with you:

  • We first capture your space and discuss your needs and desires together.
  • We present to you based on a three-dimensional design alternative proposals for the improvement of the functionality and aesthetics of your space and we modify them according to your suggestions.
  • Determine the budget of the renovation and proceed to the signing of the relevant contracts
  • We implement all the required studies and take the necessary steps to issue a building permit.
  • We finalize the work schedule
  • We renovate your space, informing you in detail per phase of work.

The offer from our company includes detailed technical descriptions of the work to be performed and alternative proposals for the materials that can be used.

Interior renovations

The interior renovation of a space aims at improving the functionality and upgrading the quality and aesthetics of the space. Renovating the interior of a building usually involves the following steps:

  • Dismantling:

    - Wall

    - Plumbing and electrical installations

    - Bathroom tiles and sanitary ware

    - Kitchen cabinets and tiles

    - Bedroom wardrobes

    - Flooring

    - Interior frames

  • Installation of new plumbing and electrical installation

  • Installation of floors

  • Installation of internal frames

  • Installation of kitchen furniture and bathroom sanitary ware

  • Oil painting of interiors

Exterior renovations

The exterior renovation of a building aims both at its energy upgrade, in order to reduce heating and cooling costs, and at its aesthetic upgrade.

It usually involves the following steps:

  • Static control of the building
  • Crack repair and plastering
  • Replacement of frames
  • Installation of thermal facade
  • Thermal and waterproofing of the roof
  • Oil painting of the building
  • Landscaping of outdoor areas
