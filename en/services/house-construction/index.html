House Construction

At Status Artis we offer comprehensive services of design, supervision and construction of turnkey houses. We create constructions with high aesthetics, functional spaces and mainly energy autonomy. We are particularly focused on low energy footprint buildings, proposing and implementing the most modern energy saving solutions.

In the context of our cooperation with you:

  • We present you alternative proposals in 3D for your new property and we modify them according to your needs and desires.
  • We determine the budget of the construction and proceed to the signing of the relevant contracts.
  • We implement all the required studies and take the necessary steps to issue a building permit.
  • We finalize the work schedule.
  • We build your property, informing you in detail per phase of work.
  • Upon completion of the project we deliver the complete file of the property.
