At Status Artis we have a 20-year presence in the construction industry with 10 years of experience in housing construction in Sweden based on energy standards applied in the Nordic countries. We always follow the new trends in construction techniques, technology and the use of innovative materials.

Why choose us

  • Quality and excellent know-how. At Status Artis we guarantee excellent results as we use quality materials from well-known companies and we have certified workshops with perfect know-how.
  • Competitive prices. We give alternative proposals and solutions based on your budget and provide a detailed account of all the work required.
  • Timely delivery of the project. We adhere to the predetermined schedule but without discounting the quality of the project.
  • Design of the space before the start of work. We capture in advanced design programs your space as it is going to be shaped so that you know its final form from the beginning.
  • Detailed employment contract. From the beginning of our cooperation you will have in your hands a work contract, which lists in detail all the work to be implemented, all the materials that will be used to perform these works and of course the schedule for their implementation.
  • Modern and specialized technical resources. In addition to the basic equipment for classic constructions, we also have machines for special metal and wood constructions (fiber laser and CNC router) for demanding customers.
  • Solving any building problem. With the modern and specialized equipment we have, we identify the exact source of the problem you are facing and we apply targeted solutions to solve it without unnecessary costs.
  • Satisfied customer. Good cooperation with you is our primary goal. That is why we carry out each construction according to your needs and desires and we are by your side with frequent information.
